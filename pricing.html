Frequently Asked Questions

Can I try Pro features before paying?

Yes! Click the "Try Pro (Demo)" button in the app to test all Pro features. When you're ready, upgrade to keep access permanently.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) and debit cards through Stripe. Enterprise customers can pay via invoice.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. Cancel anytime with one click. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period, with no questions asked.

What happens to my exports if I downgrade?

All previously exported files remain yours forever. You'll just be limited to free tier features for new exports.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 14-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied, we'll refund your purchase—no questions asked.

Is there a student or nonprofit discount?

Yes! We offer 50% off Pro plans for students and nonprofits. Contact us with verification and we'll set you up.
